Order

The Court finds in favour of the Claimant.

The Court is satisfied on the balance of probabilities that the claim for breach of duty for failure to pay salary whilst overnight should succeed. The Claimant is awarded £2912.00 for missed pay for the last 12 months.

T he Claimant is entitled to receive payment for the overnight work whilst she is lone in the future. The Defendant to pay this sum within 14 days from the date of this Order.

DJ Smyth, 1st July 2017

Costs

Defendant to pay costs of £455.00 within 14 days of the date of this Order.
